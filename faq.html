What is KoreAmigo?

KoreAmigo is a comprehensive support service designed for foreigners living in or visiting Korea. We help you navigate the complexities of Korean life, from language barriers to administrative procedures, housing, healthcare, education, and more. Our goal is to make your experience in Korea as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

Who can use KoreAmigo services?

KoreAmigo services are available to all foreigners in Korea, regardless of their nationality, visa status, or length of stay. Whether you're a tourist visiting for a few days, an international student studying for a semester, or an expatriate working in Korea for several years, we have service options tailored to your needs.

What languages do you support?

Our primary service languages are English and Korean. However, we also offer support in Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, and Thai. Our team includes multilingual professionals who can assist you in your preferred language. For less common languages, we can arrange for specialized translation services with advance notice.

How is KoreAmigo different from other services?

KoreAmigo stands out with our comprehensive approach to foreigner support. Unlike services that focus on just one aspect (like translation or housing), we provide end-to-end assistance across all areas of life in Korea. Our team has extensive experience supporting foreigners, and we combine personal service with technology to deliver efficient, high-quality support. We also offer subscription-based models that make our services more accessible and affordable compared to hiring individual service providers.

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • Housing support (property search, contract review, utility setup)
  • Visa and documentation assistance
  • Healthcare assistance (appointment scheduling, medical interpretation)
  • Education support for students and families
  • Translation and interpretation services
  • Administrative procedures assistance
  • Cultural experiences and networking
  • Emergency support

For a detailed description of each service, please visit our Services page.

Do you provide emergency assistance?

Yes, emergency assistance is available 24/7 for our Premium members. This includes help with urgent medical situations, lost documents, emergency accommodation needs, and other critical issues. Standard members can access emergency services for an additional fee. Basic (free) members can still access our emergency resources and guides, but personalized assistance is not included.

Can you help with apartment hunting and rental contracts?

Absolutely! Our housing support service is one of our most popular offerings. We help you search for properties based on your requirements, arrange viewings, negotiate with landlords/agents, review and explain rental contracts, assist with the move-in process, and help set up utilities. We can also provide guidance on Korean housing customs and rental practices to avoid common pitfalls.

Do you provide visa application assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance services. This includes guidance on visa requirements, document preparation and translation, application form assistance, and in some cases, accompaniment to immigration offices. We can help with initial visa applications, extensions, changes of visa status, and permanent residency applications. Please note that while we provide assistance, the final decision on visa issuance is solely at the discretion of Korean immigration authorities.

How much do your services cost?

We offer three membership tiers:

  • Basic (Free): Access to essential information resources, community forum, and basic translation tools.
  • Standard (39,000₩/month): Includes document translation (up to 5 pages/month), phone support during business hours, administrative assistance, and 2 hours of service accompaniment per month.
  • Premium (99,000₩/month): Includes advanced translation services, 8 hours of administrative services, 4 hours of accompaniment service, VIP cultural experiences, 24/7 emergency support, and personalized concierge service.

Annual subscriptions are available with a 16% discount. For detailed pricing information, please visit our Pricing page.

Do you offer pay-per-service options?

While our subscription model offers the best value for most users, we do understand that some people may only need assistance once or twice. For these cases, we offer pay-per-service options for most of our services. The pricing varies depending on the service type, complexity, and duration. Please contact us directly for a quote on specific one-time services.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. For monthly subscriptions, cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing period. For annual subscriptions, you can continue to use the service until the end of your paid period. We do not offer prorated refunds for unused portions of your subscription period.

Do you offer corporate or group rates?

Yes, we offer special rates for businesses, educational institutions, and groups. Our corporate packages can be customized to fit your organization's specific needs, whether you're a company with foreign employees, a university with international students, or a tour group operator. For more information on corporate and group rates, please contact our B2B team at b2b@koreamigo.com.

How do I sign up for KoreAmigo services?

Signing up is easy! Just click the "Sign Up" or "Get Started" button on our website and follow these steps:

  1. Create an account with your email address
  2. Complete your profile with basic information
  3. Select your membership plan
  4. Add payment information (for paid plans)
  5. Start using our services immediately!

The entire process takes less than 5 minutes, and you can begin accessing our resources right away.

Can I use your services before I arrive in Korea?

Absolutely! Many of our clients begin using our services well before they arrive in Korea. We can help with pre-arrival preparations such as visa applications, housing searches, travel planning, and cultural orientation. Starting early gives you a smoother transition when you arrive. Our digital platform allows you to access information and communicate with our team from anywhere in the world.

How quickly can you respond to my requests?

Our standard response time for non-emergency inquiries is within 2 business hours during our operating hours (9:00 AM to 6:00 PM KST, Monday through Friday). For Premium members, we aim to respond within 30 minutes during business hours and provide 24/7 support for urgent matters. The time to complete your request depends on its complexity, but we always strive to provide efficient service and keep you updated on our progress.

How do I request specific services?

Once you're registered, you can request services through:

  • Our user-friendly dashboard on the website
  • The KoreAmigo mobile app
  • Email at support@koreamigo.com
  • Phone at +82-10-1234-5678 (during business hours)

Each request is assigned to a qualified team member who will guide you through the entire process.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored on secure servers. We follow industry-standard security practices and comply with Korean personal information protection laws. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent, except when required by law. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, the KoreAmigo mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. The app provides all the functionality of our web platform in a convenient mobile format. You can request services, access resources, communicate with our team, and manage your account from anywhere. The app also includes offline access to essential information and translation tools, which can be particularly useful when traveling.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including:

  • Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Korean payment systems (KakaoPay, NaverPay)
  • International payment platforms (PayPal)
  • Bank transfers (for annual subscriptions)

All transactions are processed securely, and we do not store your complete credit card information on our servers.

How can I change my account settings or update my profile?

You can easily update your account settings through the "My Account" or "Profile" section on our website or mobile app. From there, you can change your personal information, update your payment details, manage your subscription, set communication preferences, and adjust notification settings. If you need assistance with account changes, our customer support team is available to help.
