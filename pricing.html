Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new pricing will take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How do I calculate the hours included in my plan?

The hours included in your plan refer to the total time our team spends providing direct assistance services for you, such as accompaniment to appointments, administrative procedures, or dedicated translation work. These hours are tracked in our system and reset at the beginning of each billing cycle.

What happens if I need more hours than my plan includes?

If you need additional assistance beyond what your plan includes, you can purchase extra hours at our standard rate of 30,000₩ per hour. We'll always notify you before you exceed your plan's hours and provide options to either upgrade your plan or purchase additional hours.

Is there a contract or commitment period?

No, all our plans are subscription-based with no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time. For monthly plans, you can cancel anytime before your next billing date. For annual plans, you can continue using the service until the end of your paid period.

Do you offer special rates for families or groups?

Yes, we offer family plans and group discounts for businesses or educational institutions. Family plans cover up to 4 family members living at the same address. For corporate or group plans, please contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and receive a customized quote.

Need a Custom Solution?

If our standard plans don't meet your specific needs, we can create a tailored solution just for you. Whether you're a business looking for employee support, an educational institution, or have unique requirements, our team is ready to help.
